About Us

Kismet Consulting, Inc. is a Civil Engineering company established July 29, 2022 by Michelle Kahl, PE. As a team of one, currently, Kismet Consulting is looking to team with other Civil firms with a goal to grow to fill more teaming needs and begin doing projects internally. With over 10 years of experience in transportation design and CADD support in a small business setting, we are well equipped to assist in both areas.

Michelle is a passionate engineer, with over 10 years of experience, striving to advance the civil transportation field utilizing appropriate technology and always looking for better, often more efficient, ways to accomplish design tasks. She enjoys being challenged and finding ways to solve difficult design problems.
